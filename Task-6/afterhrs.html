Experience the magic of Disney After Hours, an exclusive event that lets you explore the park like never before. As the sun sets and the stars come out, Disney World transforms into a wonderland of nighttime enchantment. With limited entry, you will enjoy shorter wait times and more opportunities to ride your favorite attractions. From thrilling roller coasters to charming character meet-and-greets, Disney After Hours offers a unique blend of excitement and nostalgia, creating unforgettable memories under the glow of the park twinkling lights.

Tickets for Disney After Hours are available for $60 per person and include access to a variety of rides and events throughout the park. Enjoy unlimited access to rides such as Space Mountain, Haunted Mansion, and Pirates of the Caribbean. Additionally, guests can indulge in complimentary snacks and beverages, including popcorn, ice cream, and select drinks.
