Step into a fairy tale world at the Princess Dinner Party, where dreams come true and magic is in the air. Join beloved Disney Princesses for an enchanting evening filled with delightful dining, captivating entertainment, and royal hospitality. Set in a beautifully decorated venue, this magical event offers a unique opportunity to meet and interact with your favorite princesses. Enjoy a gourmet meal fit for royalty while being serenaded with live music and enchanting stories. It's an unforgettable experience that will leave you feeling like a true prince or princess.

Tickets for the Princess Dinner Party are available for $40 per person, offering a royal dining experience that includes a multi-course meal, photo opportunities with the princesses, and a special keepsake to remember the evening. Guests can choose from a variety of menu options, including vegetarian and allergy-friendly choices, ensuring everyone can partake in the feast. The event takes place nightly, with seating times at 5:00 PM and 7:30 PM. Be sure to book your tickets early, as this popular event tends to sell out quickly. Get ready for an evening of elegance, enchantment, and endless magical moments.
