Immerse yourself in the enchanting world of Mickey's Parade, a captivating event that brings the magic of Disney to life like never before. Join Mickey, Minnie, and all your favorite characters as they parade down Main Street in a spectacle of color, music, and excitement. From the vibrant floats to the dazzling costumes, every moment of the parade is filled with wonder and joy. Don't miss your chance to experience this unforgettable celebration of imagination and creativity.

Tickets for Mickey's Parade are available for $20 per person and include access to prime viewing areas along the parade route. Guests can choose from a variety of ticket options, including standard admission and VIP packages that offer exclusive perks such as reserved seating and character meet-and-greets. The parade runs daily, with multiple showtimes to accommodate different schedules. Be sure to arrive early to secure your spot and get ready to be swept away by the magic of Disney's most iconic parade.
